Missing Playlist Song Tracker

Use this application to identify missing songs from a given YouTube playlist by comparing it to an old version of the playlist stored on your device.

Note: Since an "original" version of a playlist is required for comparison, you must first query a playlist and Register it (download it as a JSON file), which you can do here. Once you have downloaded registries of any playlist you wish to track, you may revisit this application any time in the future and query your playlist again if you notice songs have been made unavailable, and then Load your original playlist registry to identify which songs are missing.

Enter username to gather your playlists. Query a playlist to review the songs to register or compare it with an uploaded playlist registry.
